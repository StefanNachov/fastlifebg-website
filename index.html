What Is FastLifeBG?

BUILT FOR
TUNED CARS

FastLifeBG is a custom live performance display that plugs directly into your car's ECU and reads real sensor data — the same data your mapper uses, the same data a dealer diagnostic tool reads.

This is not a generic OBD reader. Every display is configured to your specific car and your specific tune. Your mapper's boost targets, your temperature limits, your gauge scaling — all matched to your actual build. 2.0 bar tune gets a 2.0 bar gauge. 4.0 bar gets 4.0 bar.

Whether you're on a mountain road, a track day, or a drag strip — FastLifeBG gives you a live window into everything your engine is doing. A tuned car deserves a tuned display.

What The Display Shows

THREE PAGES.
EVERYTHING YOU NEED.

The display is structured into three focused screens. Switch between them while driving — each built for a specific purpose.

Real Boost Pressure
Live MAP sensor data converted to gauge bar, updated multiple times per second. You see what your turbo is actually doing.
Boost Target vs Actual
What your ECU is requesting against what it's achieving. See boost deviation at a glance.
VNT Vane Position %
Watch your variable nozzle turbo working in real time. Essential for understanding spool and boost response on variable-geometry turbos.
Coolant Temperature
With custom warning thresholds matched to your specific engine — not generic defaults that mean nothing for your build.
Intercooler Air Temp (IAT)
Post-intercooler temperature. Essential for mapped cars and water spray setups. Shows seconds since last update — watch the temperature drop in real time when you spray.
Fuel Temperature
Critical on high-power PD and common rail diesel builds where fuel heating directly affects delivery and power.
Injection Quantity (mg/stroke)
See exactly how hard your injectors are working. Compare against your mapper's targets in real time.
Engine Load % + RPM
ECU-calculated load and RPM, always visible. The master reference numbers your ECU uses to decide everything it does.
Fault Codes — Fully Decoded
Not just "P0101" — full descriptions like "MAF Sensor — Circuit Performance." Know exactly what your ECU is flagging without plugging in a separate diagnostic tool.
Per-Cylinder Injection Balance
See how your ECU is balancing fuel delivery across all cylinders at idle. Uneven cylinders reveal worn injectors before they become expensive failures.
Battery Voltage
Live alternator and battery health. Catch charging faults before they strand you.
Instant Fuel Consumption (L/H)
Litres per hour direct from your ECU — actual injector delivery, not a calculation from an OBD estimate.
Calculated L/100km
Real fuel consumption derived from live speed and flow data.
Rolling Average Consumption
Trip average since you started the engine. Useful for long runs and comparing driving styles.
0–100 km/h Timer
Automatically triggered when you launch, stopped at your actual 100 km/h. Includes a configurable speedometer correction factor — typically 5% — so the timer triggers at real speed, not what your speedo says.
100–200 km/h Pull Timer
Records the rolling pull from 100 to 200 km/h — the real measure of top-end power. Same correction factor applied. Results comparable to professional timing equipment like Dragy.
Peak Boost — Session
Highest boost recorded since the last reset. Held on screen until you clear it.
Peak RPM — Session
Highest RPM seen this session. Held until reset.
Live Speed Display
Large, clear speed reading during the run. No phone, no Dragy, no assistant needed.
Reset & Run Again
Results held on screen after each run. Press the button to clear and go again.

Your Warning Thresholds
Green, amber, and red thresholds are calibrated to your engine's actual limits — not defaults that either panic you unnecessarily or let you cook your build without warning.
Speedometer Correction

In Development

WHAT'S NEXT.

Features currently being developed for future FastLifeBG versions.

Boost Build Graph

A live X/Y graph of boost pressure vs RPM, drawn in real time as you drive. See exactly where your turbo spools, where peak boost hits, where it falls off at the top. Compare runs, identify boost creep, verify your mapper's targets are met across the full rev range.

Automatic Gearbox Monitoring

A dedicated transmission health page for automatic builds: fluid temperature, gear position, torque converter lockup, slip detection, and adaptation values. High ATF temperature is the number one cause of automatic gearbox failure on tuned cars. Having it visible means you can protect a very expensive part by watching one number.

Transmission Shift Graph

For automatic gearboxes — a real-time graph of hydraulic pressure during each gear change. Identify soft shifts, hard shifts, and slipping. The same data professional transmission tuners use, available while you drive.
