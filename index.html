What Is FastLifeBG?

REAL DATA.
YOUR CAR.
YOUR TUNE.

FastLifeBG connects directly to your car's ECU and reads actual sensor values — the same data your mapper sees, the same data a dealer diagnostic tool reads. Not estimates. Not OBD averages.

Every display is built for one specific car and one specific protocol. The VAG TDI display speaks KW1281. The BMW display listens on PT-CAN. The Golf R display polls via UDS over CAN FD. Three completely different engineering approaches — because that's what it takes to do it properly.

Your boost gauge is scaled to your actual target. Your warning thresholds match your engine's real limits. A tuned car deserves a tuned display.

How It Works

BUILT FOR YOUR
SPECIFIC CAR.

01
Order & Configure

Tell us your car, your engine, and your tune target. We configure the display to your boost scale, your warning thresholds, and your colour theme before it ships.

02
Direct ECU Connection

The display connects to your car's native protocol — K-Line, PT-CAN, or CAN FD depending on the car. No OBD2 adapters, no Bluetooth lag, no approximations.

03
Live While You Drive

Boost, temperatures, injection data, cylinder balance, rail pressure, DSG fluid — all updating in real time. Colour-coded alerts when values exceed your thresholds.

04
Know Before It Breaks

Cylinder imbalance spotted before the injector fails. Rail pressure drop flagged before the pump goes. Transmission fluid creeping up before the gearbox cooks. That's the point.
